Rap-style Comment Generation in an Audience Participation Fighting Game

  This paper presents a rap-style commentary system that enhances Enjoyment in an audience participation online live-streaming fighting game. Game live streaming has been rapidly growing, and "audience participation games (APGs)" is a style of games continuously receiving more attentions from game researchers and the game industry; it allows audiences to participate in, or interact with, the game being streamed in a meaningful way, leading to better experience and promoted social connectivity.

  However, there exist some common problems and limitations in many twitch channels, which are that humans cannot provide content for 24 hours, and that game commentary (known so far for its capability to enhance audience experience) may not be adequately provided. musical-like Commentary Mechanism is introduced by our group as a solution, with supporting studies that Rap-style of music has therapeutic benefits, making it more powerful than normal speech commentary.

  This paper describes design to make an existing fighting game become an APG through the process of AI's strength adjustment based on audiences chat messages, and how to apply such strength adjustment to generate game commentary. The result shows that the proposed system not only promote audience experience, but also make them more active to chat massages.
